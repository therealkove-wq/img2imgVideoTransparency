# Prompt Examples for Wan-Alpha Transparent Video Generation

## Prompt Structure

The most effective prompts follow this structure:

```
[Transparency statement]. [Shot type]. [Main subject and action]. [Visual style]. [Additional details].
```

## Tips for Better Results

### ✨ Transparency Clarity
- **Always include** one of these phrases:
  - "This video has a transparent background"
  - "The background is transparent"
  - "Transparent background"
  - "Background should be transparent"

### 🎬 Shot Type (Choose One)
- Close-up (extreme detail)
- Medium shot (balanced view)
- Wide shot (shows full scene)
- Full body shot (for characters)
- Pan shot (camera moves)

### 🎨 Visual Style Options
- Realistic
- Artistic
- Cinematic
- Cartoon / Animated
- 3D rendered
- Minimal
- Fantasy
- Futuristic

### ⭐ Quality Modifiers
- Sharp and clear
- High quality
- Detailed
- Smooth
- Crisp
- Professional
- Fine-grained details

### Multiple Objects
```

```

### Detailed Description
```
The background is transparent. Medium shot. A mechanical gear system rotating smoothly. 
Silver and brass colors with fine details. Intricate design with multiple interlocking gears. 
Industrial style, precise movement. High quality, sharp details.
```

## Prompts That Don't Work Well

❌ Avoid:
- No mention of transparency → background will not be transparent
- Too vague ("make a video") → results will be random
- Conflicting information ("transparent AND white background")
- Complex scenes that are hard to visualize
- Multiple unrelated subjects without composition guidance

---

## Format Checklist

Before running your prompt, check:

✓ Includes transparency statement
✓ Specifies shot type
✓ Describes main subject clearly
✓ Includes visual style
✓ Has quality descriptors
✓ Is 20-50 words (optimal length)
✓ Uses clear, descriptive language

## Customization Guide

Modify these templates with your own values:

```
[TRANSPARENCY]: This video has a transparent background
[SHOT]: Medium shot
[SUBJECT]: A rotating golden sphere
[STYLE]: Realistic, cinematic lighting
[DETAILS]: with reflections and subtle shadows

Result: "This video has a transparent background. Medium shot. A rotating golden sphere. 
Realistic, cinematic lighting, with reflections and subtle shadows."
```

## Notes

- Transparency quality improves with:
  - Explicit transparency mention
  - Clearer subject descriptions
  - Higher step counts (4-8 recommended)
  - Better seed selection
  
- Experiment with different seeds if results aren't satisfactory
- Longer prompts (40-60 words) often produce better results
- Be specific about colors and materials for better quality
